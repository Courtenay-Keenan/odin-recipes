Overnight Oatmeal

Description

Loaded with calcium, protein and fiber, this oatmeal will keep in the fridge up to 2 days. Enjoy this dairy-free treat for breakfast for a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1/4 cup low-fat, plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup diced apples or rasins
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Bring almond milk to almost to a boil in a small pot
  2. Remove from heat, then add remaining ingredients
  3. Cover, then allow to cool to room temperature, about 15 minutes
  4. Refrigerate overnight and eat chilled
